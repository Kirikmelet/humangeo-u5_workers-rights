Where The Unions Be?

During the Cold War, most left-wing unions faded into obscurity, however some do still exist today. Moderate unions like the AFL-CIO still exist and are still kicking today. However, anti-communism and "Right To Work Laws" have diminished the power and recruitment of unions. Membership of unions have decreased from 20% of all worker's in 1983 to around 13%. While public-sector union membership increased, private sector unions decreased quickly. Large companies like Amazon, Tesla, Google, discourage union drives and regularly intimidate and post anti-union propaganda.
