A Summary of the Project

Unions have formed an integral part of American society. Else without them we wouldn't have the worker's rights we enjoy today. A couple of the oldest unions in America still exist today. The road to labor rights was marked by violence and death. A multitude of casualties were recorded in strikes that turned deadly between the state and the union. While the legacy of programs made to support labor unions and enfore labor rights stand strong, a couple were either pulled back or reversed. We can still strengthen labor unions and wrangle more reforms from the government.
